# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Instagram Sales Agent / sihatukn8n-IG automation handles information received through Instagram messages and related Meta services.

## 1. Purpose of the Service

This service is used to reply to Instagram direct messages, answer customer questions, collect order details, notify the store owner, and save order information for fulfillment.

## 2. Information We May Collect

When a user messages the connected Instagram account, the service may process the following information:

* Instagram user identifier provided by Meta
* Message text sent by the user
* Order details voluntarily provided by the user, such as name, phone number, delivery address, requested product, and notes
* Message metadata required for automation, such as message ID and timestamp
* Attachments or voice messages only when the user sends them to the connected Instagram account

## 3. How Information Is Used

The information is used only to:

* Respond to customer messages
* Answer product, delivery, and order questions
* Create and manage customer orders
* Notify the store owner or support operator
* Prevent duplicate message processing and basic abuse
* Improve the reliability of the automation workflow

## 4. Data Sharing

Customer information is not sold.

Information may be shared only with:

* The store owner or authorized operator for order handling and customer support
* Service providers used to run the automation, such as Meta, n8n, Google Sheets, Telegram, OpenAI, and database or hosting providers
* Authorities if required by applicable law

## 5. Data Storage

Order and message-processing data may be stored in tools such as Google Sheets, n8n data tables, or database services used by the workflow.

Data is kept only as long as needed for order handling, support, security, and operational records.

## 6. User Choices

Users may choose not to provide personal information.

If required order details such as phone number or delivery address are not provided, the store may be unable to complete the order.

Users may request deletion or correction of their order information by contacting the store through the same Instagram account they used to place the order.

## 7. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

## 8. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for general customer communication and is not directed to children. The service does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

## 10. Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact the store through the connected Instagram account or through the GitHub account that hosts this policy.
